Main Page

In each of the selected chapters, there will be at most 3 types of elements.

  1. Quiz: An ordinary type of quiz, computer marks the answers automatically.
  2. Self-test: Quizzes that involves long questions, you will be asked to mark the questions yourselves. It is a good tool for memorizing.
  3. Resources: Knowledge about chemistry, some may include answers of the quizzes. If you are interested in the copyright details, view this page.

Click on them to start the quiz or view the resource!

Use the quick navigation tool to get to your desired quiz quickly.

Auto Quiz

This is the quiz interface. The top part shows your score, the middle part contains the question and input textboxes, the bottom part shows the answer of the previous question and the instructions.

Type in your answer and press 'next' or press the enter key on your keyboard.

After that, the score will be automatically updated at the top of the page (Please note that the total score for each question is 1, like in the example, each blank contributes 0.5 mark.

You can also see the details of the previous question here.

Self Test

This is the self test interface, which is similar to auto quiz ones.

Press 'check answer' after you have finished.

Some blanks that can be automatically checked will be checked now, correct answer will be shown.

Long questions will appear yellow, choose the mark it deserves according to the correct answer. After you have finished, click 'next'

The status bar will update itself.
